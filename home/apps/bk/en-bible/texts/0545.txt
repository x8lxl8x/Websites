# Psalms 67

<p class="intro">To the chief Musician on Neginoth, A Psalm <i>or</i> Song.</p>

<p>%[l|1]&ensp; God be merciful unto us, and bless us; <i>and</i> cause his face to shine upon us; Selah.</p>

<p>%[l|2]&ensp; That thy way may be known upon earth, thy saving health among all nations.</p>

<p>%[l|3]&ensp; Let the people praise thee, O God; let all the people praise thee.</p>

<p>%[l|4]&ensp; O let the nations be glad and sing for joy: for thou shalt judge the people righteously, and govern the nations upon earth. Selah.</p>

<p>%[l|5]&ensp; Let the people praise thee, O God; let all the people praise thee.</p>

<p>%[l|6]&ensp; <i>Then</i> shall the earth yield her increase; <i>and</i> God, <i>even</i> our own God, shall bless us.</p>

<p>%[l|7]&ensp; God shall bless us; and all the ends of the earth shall fear him.</p>
