# Nehemiah 11

<p>%[l|1]&ensp; And the rulers of the people dwelt at Jerusalem: the rest of the people also cast lots, to bring one of ten to dwell in Jerusalem the holy city, and nine parts <i>to dwell</i> in <i>other</i> cities.</p>

<p>%[l|2]&ensp; And the people blessed all the men, that willingly offered themselves to dwell at Jerusalem.</p>

<p>%[l|3]&ensp; Now these <i>are</i> the chief of the province that dwelt in Jerusalem: but in the cities of Judah dwelt every one in his possession in their cities, <i>to wit</i>, Israel, the priests, and the Levites, and the Nethinims, and the children of Solomon's servants.</p>

<p>%[l|4]&ensp; And at Jerusalem dwelt <i>certain</i> of the children of Judah, and of the children of Benjamin. Of the children of Judah; Athaiah the son of Uzziah, the son of Zechariah, the son of Amariah, the son of Shephatiah, the son of Mahalaleel, of the children of Perez;</p>

<p>%[l|5]&ensp; And Maaseiah the son of Baruch, the son of Colhozeh, the son of Hazaiah, the son of Adaiah, the son of Joiarib, the son of Zechariah, the son of Shiloni.</p>

<p>%[l|6]&ensp; All the sons of Perez that dwelt at Jerusalem <i>were</i> four hundred threescore and eight valiant men.</p>

<p>%[l|7]&ensp; And these <i>are</i> the sons of Benjamin; Sallu the son of Meshullam, the son of Joed, the son of Pedaiah, the son of Kolaiah, the son of Maaseiah, the son of Ithiel, the son of Jesaiah.</p>

<p>%[l|8]&ensp; And after him Gabbai, Sallai, nine hundred twenty and eight.</p>

<p>%[l|9]&ensp; And Joel the son of Zichri <i>was</i> their overseer: and Judah the son of Senuah <i>was</i> second over the city.</p>

<p>%[l|10]&ensp; Of the priests: Jedaiah the son of Joiarib, Jachin.</p>

<p>%[l|11]&ensp; Seraiah the son of Hilkiah, the son of Meshullam, the son of Zadok, the son of Meraioth, the son of Ahitub, <i>was</i> the ruler of the house of God.</p>

<p>%[l|12]&ensp; And their brethren that did the work of the house <i>were</i> eight hundred twenty and two: and Adaiah the son of Jeroham, the son of Pelaliah, the son of Amzi, the son of Zechariah, the son of Pashur, the son of Malchiah,</p>

<p>%[l|13]&ensp; And his brethren, chief of the fathers, two hundred forty and two: and Amashai the son of Azareel, the son of Ahasai, the son of Meshillemoth, the son of Immer,</p>

<p>%[l|14]&ensp; And their brethren, mighty men of valour, an hundred twenty and eight: and their overseer <i>was</i> Zabdiel, the son of <i>one of</i> the great men.</p>

<p>%[l|15]&ensp; Also of the Levites: Shemaiah the son of Hashub, the son of Azrikam, the son of Hashabiah, the son of Bunni;</p>

<p>%[l|16]&ensp; And Shabbethai and Jozabad, of the chief of the Levites, <i>had</i> the oversight of the outward business of the house of God.</p>

<p>%[l|17]&ensp; And Mattaniah the son of Micha, the son of Zabdi, the son of Asaph, <i>was</i> the principal to begin the thanksgiving in prayer: and Bakbukiah the second among his brethren, and Abda the son of Shammua, the son of Galal, the son of Jeduthun.</p>

<p>%[l|18]&ensp; All the Levites in the holy city <i>were</i> two hundred fourscore and four.</p>

<p>%[l|19]&ensp; Moreover the porters, Akkub, Talmon, and their brethren that kept the gates, <i>were</i> an hundred seventy and two.</p>

<p>%[l|20]&ensp; And the residue of Israel, of the priests, <i>and</i> the Levites, <i>were</i> in all the cities of Judah, every one in his inheritance.</p>

<p>%[l|21]&ensp; But the Nethinims dwelt in Ophel: and Ziha and Gispa <i>were</i> over the Nethinims.</p>

<p>%[l|22]&ensp; The overseer also of the Levites at Jerusalem <i>was</i> Uzzi the son of Bani, the son of Hashabiah, the son of Mattaniah, the son of Micha. Of the sons of Asaph, the singers <i>were</i> over the business of the house of God.</p>

<p>%[l|23]&ensp; For <i>it was</i> the king's commandment concerning them, that a certain portion should be for the singers, due for every day.</p>

<p>%[l|24]&ensp; And Pethahiah the son of Meshezabeel, of the children of Zerah the son of Judah, <i>was</i> at the king's hand in all matters concerning the people.</p>

<p>%[l|25]&ensp; And for the villages, with their fields, <i>some</i> of the children of Judah dwelt at Kirjatharba, and <i>in</i> the villages thereof, and at Dibon, and <i>in</i> the villages thereof, and at Jekabzeel, and <i>in</i> the villages thereof,</p>

<p>%[l|26]&ensp; And at Jeshua, and at Moladah, and at Bethphelet,</p>

<p>%[l|27]&ensp; And at Hazarshual, and at Beersheba, and <i>in</i> the villages thereof,</p>

<p>%[l|28]&ensp; And at Ziklag, and at Mekonah, and in the villages thereof,</p>

<p>%[l|29]&ensp; And at Enrimmon, and at Zareah, and at Jarmuth,</p>

<p>%[l|30]&ensp; Zanoah, Adullam, and <i>in</i> their villages, at Lachish, and the fields thereof, at Azekah, and <i>in</i> the villages thereof. And they dwelt from Beersheba unto the valley of Hinnom.</p>

<p>%[l|31]&ensp; The children also of Benjamin from Geba <i>dwelt</i> at Michmash, and Aija, and Bethel, and <i>in</i> their villages,</p>

<p>%[l|32]&ensp; <i>And</i> at Anathoth, Nob, Ananiah,</p>

<p>%[l|33]&ensp; Hazor, Ramah, Gittaim,</p>

<p>%[l|34]&ensp; Hadid, Zeboim, Neballat,</p>

<p>%[l|35]&ensp; Lod, and Ono, the valley of craftsmen.</p>

<p>%[l|36]&ensp; And of the Levites <i>were</i> divisions <i>in</i> Judah, <i>and</i> in Benjamin.</p>
