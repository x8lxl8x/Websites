# Psalms 136

<p>%[l|1]&ensp; O give thanks unto the LORD; for <i>he is</i> good: for his mercy <i>endureth</i> for ever.</p>

<p>%[l|2]&ensp; O give thanks unto the God of gods: for his mercy <i>endureth</i> for ever.</p>

<p>%[l|3]&ensp; O give thanks to the Lord of lords: for his mercy <i>endureth</i> for ever.</p>

<p>%[l|4]&ensp; To him who alone doeth great wonders: for his mercy <i>endureth</i> for ever.</p>

<p>%[l|5]&ensp; To him that by wisdom made the heavens: for his mercy <i>endureth</i> for ever.</p>

<p>%[l|6]&ensp; To him that stretched out the earth above the waters: for his mercy <i>endureth</i> for ever.</p>

<p>%[l|7]&ensp; To him that made great lights: for his mercy <i>endureth</i> for ever:</p>

<p>%[l|8]&ensp; The sun to rule by day: for his mercy <i>endureth</i> for ever:</p>

<p>%[l|9]&ensp; The moon and stars to rule by night: for his mercy <i>endureth</i> for ever.</p>

<p>%[l|10]&ensp; To him that smote Egypt in their firstborn: for his mercy <i>endureth</i> for ever:</p>

<p>%[l|11]&ensp; And brought out Israel from among them: for his mercy <i>endureth</i> for ever:</p>

<p>%[l|12]&ensp; With a strong hand, and with a stretched out arm: for his mercy <i>endureth</i> for ever.</p>

<p>%[l|13]&ensp; To him which divided the Red sea into parts: for his mercy <i>endureth</i> for ever:</p>

<p>%[l|14]&ensp; And made Israel to pass through the midst of it: for his mercy <i>endureth</i> for ever:</p>

<p>%[l|15]&ensp; But overthrew Pharaoh and his host in the Red sea: for his mercy <i>endureth</i> for ever.</p>

<p>%[l|16]&ensp; To him which led his people through the wilderness: for his mercy <i>endureth</i> for ever.</p>

<p>%[l|17]&ensp; To him which smote great kings: for his mercy <i>endureth</i> for ever:</p>

<p>%[l|18]&ensp; And slew famous kings: for his mercy <i>endureth</i> for ever:</p>

<p>%[l|19]&ensp; Sihon king of the Amorites: for his mercy <i>endureth</i> for ever:</p>

<p>%[l|20]&ensp; And Og the king of Bashan: for his mercy <i>endureth</i> for ever:</p>

<p>%[l|21]&ensp; And gave their land for an heritage: for his mercy <i>endureth</i> for ever:</p>

<p>%[l|22]&ensp; <i>Even</i> an heritage unto Israel his servant: for his mercy <i>endureth</i> for ever.</p>

<p>%[l|23]&ensp; Who remembered us in our low estate: for his mercy <i>endureth</i> for ever:</p>

<p>%[l|24]&ensp; And hath redeemed us from our enemies: for his mercy <i>endureth</i> for ever.</p>

<p>%[l|25]&ensp; Who giveth food to all flesh: for his mercy <i>endureth</i> for ever.</p>

<p>%[l|26]&ensp; O give thanks unto the God of heaven: for his mercy <i>endureth</i> for ever.</p>
