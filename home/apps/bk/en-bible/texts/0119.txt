# Numbers 2

<p>%[l|1]&ensp; And the LORD spake unto Moses and unto Aaron, saying,</p>

<p>%[l|2]&ensp; Every man of the children of Israel shall pitch by his own standard, with the ensign of their father's house: far off about the tabernacle of the congregation shall they pitch.</p>

<p>%[l|3]&ensp; And on the east side toward the rising of the sun shall they of the standard of the camp of Judah pitch throughout their armies: and Nahshon the son of Amminadab <i>shall be</i> captain of the children of Judah.</p>

<p>%[l|4]&ensp; And his host, and those that were numbered of them, <i>were</i> threescore and fourteen thousand and six hundred.</p>

<p>%[l|5]&ensp; And those that do pitch next unto him <i>shall be</i> the tribe of Issachar: and Nethaneel the son of Zuar <i>shall be</i> captain of the children of Issachar.</p>

<p>%[l|6]&ensp; And his host, and those that were numbered thereof, <i>were</i> fifty and four thousand and four hundred.</p>

<p>%[l|7]&ensp; <i>Then</i> the tribe of Zebulun: and Eliab the son of Helon <i>shall be</i> captain of the children of Zebulun.</p>

<p>%[l|8]&ensp; And his host, and those that were numbered thereof, <i>were</i> fifty and seven thousand and four hundred.</p>

<p>%[l|9]&ensp; All that were numbered in the camp of Judah <i>were</i> an hundred thousand and fourscore thousand and six thousand and four hundred, throughout their armies. These shall first set forth.</p>

<p>%[l|10]&ensp; On the south side <i>shall be</i> the standard of the camp of Reuben according to their armies: and the captain of the children of Reuben <i>shall be</i> Elizur the son of Shedeur.</p>

<p>%[l|11]&ensp; And his host, and those that were numbered thereof, <i>were</i> forty and six thousand and five hundred.</p>

<p>%[l|12]&ensp; And those which pitch by him <i>shall be</i> the tribe of Simeon: and the captain of the children of Simeon <i>shall be</i> Shelumiel the son of Zurishaddai.</p>

<p>%[l|13]&ensp; And his host, and those that were numbered of them, <i>were</i> fifty and nine thousand and three hundred.</p>

<p>%[l|14]&ensp; Then the tribe of Gad: and the captain of the sons of Gad <i>shall be</i> Eliasaph the son of Reuel.</p>

<p>%[l|15]&ensp; And his host, and those that were numbered of them, <i>were</i> forty and five thousand and six hundred and fifty.</p>

<p>%[l|16]&ensp; All that were numbered in the camp of Reuben <i>were</i> an hundred thousand and fifty and one thousand and four hundred and fifty, throughout their armies. And they shall set forth in the second rank.</p>

<p>%[l|17]&ensp; Then the tabernacle of the congregation shall set forward with the camp of the Levites in the midst of the camp: as they encamp, so shall they set forward, every man in his place by their standards.</p>

<p>%[l|18]&ensp; On the west side <i>shall be</i> the standard of the camp of Ephraim according to their armies: and the captain of the sons of Ephraim <i>shall be</i> Elishama the son of Ammihud.</p>

<p>%[l|19]&ensp; And his host, and those that were numbered of them, <i>were</i> forty thousand and five hundred.</p>

<p>%[l|20]&ensp; And by him <i>shall be</i> the tribe of Manasseh: and the captain of the children of Manasseh <i>shall be</i> Gamaliel the son of Pedahzur.</p>

<p>%[l|21]&ensp; And his host, and those that were numbered of them, <i>were</i> thirty and two thousand and two hundred.</p>

<p>%[l|22]&ensp; Then the tribe of Benjamin: and the captain of the sons of Benjamin <i>shall be</i> Abidan the son of Gideoni.</p>

<p>%[l|23]&ensp; And his host, and those that were numbered of them, <i>were</i> thirty and five thousand and four hundred.</p>

<p>%[l|24]&ensp; All that were numbered of the camp of Ephraim <i>were</i> an hundred thousand and eight thousand and an hundred, throughout their armies. And they shall go forward in the third rank.</p>

<p>%[l|25]&ensp; The standard of the camp of Dan <i>shall be</i> on the north side by their armies: and the captain of the children of Dan <i>shall be</i> Ahiezer the son of Ammishaddai.</p>

<p>%[l|26]&ensp; And his host, and those that were numbered of them, <i>were</i> threescore and two thousand and seven hundred.</p>

<p>%[l|27]&ensp; And those that encamp by him <i>shall be</i> the tribe of Asher: and the captain of the children of Asher <i>shall be</i> Pagiel the son of Ocran.</p>

<p>%[l|28]&ensp; And his host, and those that were numbered of them, <i>were</i> forty and one thousand and five hundred.</p>

<p>%[l|29]&ensp; Then the tribe of Naphtali: and the captain of the children of Naphtali <i>shall be</i> Ahira the son of Enan.</p>

<p>%[l|30]&ensp; And his host, and those that were numbered of them, <i>were</i> fifty and three thousand and four hundred.</p>

<p>%[l|31]&ensp; All they that were numbered in the camp of Dan <i>were</i> an hundred thousand and fifty and seven thousand and six hundred. They shall go hindmost with their standards.</p>

<p>%[l|32]&ensp; These <i>are</i> those which were numbered of the children of Israel by the house of their fathers: all those that were numbered of the camps throughout their hosts <i>were</i> six hundred thousand and three thousand and five hundred and fifty.</p>

<p>%[l|33]&ensp; But the Levites were not numbered among the children of Israel; as the LORD commanded Moses.</p>

<p>%[l|34]&ensp; And the children of Israel did according to all that the LORD commanded Moses: so they pitched by their standards, and so they set forward, every one after their families, according to the house of their fathers.</p>
