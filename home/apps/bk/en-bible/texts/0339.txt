## I Chronicles ##
# I Chronicles 1

<p>%[l|1]&ensp; Adam, Sheth, Enosh,</p>

<p>%[l|2]&ensp; Kenan, Mahalaleel, Jered,</p>

<p>%[l|3]&ensp; Henoch, Methuselah, Lamech,</p>

<p>%[l|4]&ensp; Noah, Shem, Ham, and Japheth.</p>

<p>%[l|5]&ensp; The sons of Japheth; Gomer, and Magog, and Madai, and Javan, and Tubal, and Meshech, and Tiras.</p>

<p>%[l|6]&ensp; And the sons of Gomer; Ashchenaz, and Riphath, and Togarmah.</p>

<p>%[l|7]&ensp; And the sons of Javan; Elishah, and Tarshish, Kittim, and Dodanim.</p>

<p>%[l|8]&ensp; The sons of Ham; Cush, and Mizraim, Put, and Canaan.</p>

<p>%[l|9]&ensp; And the sons of Cush; Seba, and Havilah, and Sabta, and Raamah, and Sabtecha. And the sons of Raamah; Sheba, and Dedan.</p>

<p>%[l|10]&ensp; And Cush begat Nimrod: he began to be mighty upon the earth.</p>

<p>%[l|11]&ensp; And Mizraim begat Ludim, and Anamim, and Lehabim, and Naphtuhim,</p>

<p>%[l|12]&ensp; And Pathrusim, and Casluhim, (of whom came the Philistines,) and Caphthorim.</p>

<p>%[l|13]&ensp; And Canaan begat Zidon his firstborn, and Heth,</p>

<p>%[l|14]&ensp; The Jebusite also, and the Amorite, and the Girgashite,</p>

<p>%[l|15]&ensp; And the Hivite, and the Arkite, and the Sinite,</p>

<p>%[l|16]&ensp; And the Arvadite, and the Zemarite, and the Hamathite.</p>

<p>%[l|17]&ensp; The sons of Shem; Elam, and Asshur, and Arphaxad, and Lud, and Aram, and Uz, and Hul, and Gether, and Meshech.</p>

<p>%[l|18]&ensp; And Arphaxad begat Shelah, and Shelah begat Eber.</p>

<p>%[l|19]&ensp; And unto Eber were born two sons: the name of the one <i>was</i> Peleg; because in his days the earth was divided: and his brother's name <i>was</i> Joktan.</p>

<p>%[l|20]&ensp; And Joktan begat Almodad, and Sheleph, and Hazarmaveth, and Jerah,</p>

<p>%[l|21]&ensp; Hadoram also, and Uzal, and Diklah,</p>

<p>%[l|22]&ensp; And Ebal, and Abimael, and Sheba,</p>

<p>%[l|23]&ensp; And Ophir, and Havilah, and Jobab. All these <i>were</i> the sons of Joktan.</p>

<p>%[l|24]&ensp; Shem, Arphaxad, Shelah,</p>

<p>%[l|25]&ensp; Eber, Peleg, Reu,</p>

<p>%[l|26]&ensp; Serug, Nahor, Terah,</p>

<p>%[l|27]&ensp; Abram; the same <i>is</i> Abraham.</p>

<p>%[l|28]&ensp; The sons of Abraham; Isaac, and Ishmael.</p>

<p>%[l|29]&ensp; These <i>are</i> their generations: The firstborn of Ishmael, Nebaioth; then Kedar, and Adbeel, and Mibsam,</p>

<p>%[l|30]&ensp; Mishma, and Dumah, Massa, Hadad, and Tema,</p>

<p>%[l|31]&ensp; Jetur, Naphish, and Kedemah. These are the sons of Ishmael.</p>

<p>%[l|32]&ensp; Now the sons of Keturah, Abraham's concubine: she bare Zimran, and Jokshan, and Medan, and Midian, and Ishbak, and Shuah. And the sons of Jokshan; Sheba, and Dedan.</p>

<p>%[l|33]&ensp; And the sons of Midian; Ephah, and Epher, and Henoch, and Abida, and Eldaah. All these <i>are</i> the sons of Keturah.</p>

<p>%[l|34]&ensp; And Abraham begat Isaac. The sons of Isaac; Esau and Israel.</p>

<p>%[l|35]&ensp; The sons of Esau; Eliphaz, Reuel, and Jeush, and Jaalam, and Korah.</p>

<p>%[l|36]&ensp; The sons of Eliphaz; Teman, and Omar, Zephi, and Gatam, Kenaz, and Timna, and Amalek.</p>

<p>%[l|37]&ensp; The sons of Reuel; Nahath, Zerah, Shammah, and Mizzah.</p>

<p>%[l|38]&ensp; And the sons of Seir; Lotan, and Shobal, and Zibeon, and Anah, and Dishon, and Ezer, and Dishan.</p>

<p>%[l|39]&ensp; And the sons of Lotan; Hori, and Homam: and Timna <i>was</i> Lotan's sister.</p>

<p>%[l|40]&ensp; The sons of Shobal; Alian, and Manahath, and Ebal, Shephi, and Onam. And the sons of Zibeon; Aiah, and Anah.</p>

<p>%[l|41]&ensp; The sons of Anah; Dishon. And the sons of Dishon; Amram, and Eshban, and Ithran, and Cheran.</p>

<p>%[l|42]&ensp; The sons of Ezer; Bilhan, and Zavan, <i>and</i> Jakan. The sons of Dishan; Uz, and Aran.</p>

<p>%[l|43]&ensp; Now these <i>are</i> the kings that reigned in the land of Edom before <i>any</i> king reigned over the children of Israel; Bela the son of Beor: and the name of his city <i>was</i> Dinhabah.</p>

<p>%[l|44]&ensp; And when Bela was dead, Jobab the son of Zerah of Bozrah reigned in his stead.</p>

<p>%[l|45]&ensp; And when Jobab was dead, Husham of the land of the Temanites reigned in his stead.</p>

<p>%[l|46]&ensp; And when Husham was dead, Hadad the son of Bedad, which smote Midian in the field of Moab, reigned in his stead: and the name of his city <i>was</i> Avith.</p>

<p>%[l|47]&ensp; And when Hadad was dead, Samlah of Masrekah reigned in his stead.</p>

<p>%[l|48]&ensp; And when Samlah was dead, Shaul of Rehoboth by the river reigned in his stead.</p>

<p>%[l|49]&ensp; And when Shaul was dead, Baalhanan the son of Achbor reigned in his stead.</p>

<p>%[l|50]&ensp; And when Baalhanan was dead, Hadad reigned in his stead: and the name of his city <i>was</i> Pai; and his wife's name <i>was</i> Mehetabel, the daughter of Matred, the daughter of Mezahab.</p>

<p>%[l|51]&ensp; Hadad died also. And the dukes of Edom were; duke Timnah, duke Aliah, duke Jetheth,</p>

<p>%[l|52]&ensp; Duke Aholibamah, duke Elah, duke Pinon,</p>

<p>%[l|53]&ensp; Duke Kenaz, duke Teman, duke Mibzar,</p>

<p>%[l|54]&ensp; Duke Magdiel, duke Iram. These <i>are</i> the dukes of Edom.</p>
