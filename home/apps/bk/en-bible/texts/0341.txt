# I Chronicles 3

<p>%[l|1]&ensp; Now these were the sons of David, which were born unto him in Hebron; the firstborn Amnon, of Ahinoam the Jezreelitess; the second Daniel, of Abigail the Carmelitess:</p>

<p>%[l|2]&ensp; The third, Absalom the son of Maachah the daughter of Talmai king of Geshur: the fourth, Adonijah the son of Haggith:</p>

<p>%[l|3]&ensp; The fifth, Shephatiah of Abital: the sixth, Ithream by Eglah his wife.</p>

<p>%[l|4]&ensp; <i>These</i> six were born unto him in Hebron; and there he reigned seven years and six months: and in Jerusalem he reigned thirty and three years.</p>

<p>%[l|5]&ensp; And these were born unto him in Jerusalem; Shimea, and Shobab, and Nathan, and Solomon, four, of Bathshua the daughter of Ammiel:</p>

<p>%[l|6]&ensp; Ibhar also, and Elishama, and Eliphelet,</p>

<p>%[l|7]&ensp; And Nogah, and Nepheg, and Japhia,</p>

<p>%[l|8]&ensp; And Elishama, and Eliada, and Eliphelet, nine.</p>

<p>%[l|9]&ensp; <i>These were</i> all the sons of David, beside the sons of the concubines, and Tamar their sister.</p>

<p>%[l|10]&ensp; And Solomon's son <i>was</i> Rehoboam, Abia his son, Asa his son, Jehoshaphat his son,</p>

<p>%[l|11]&ensp; Joram his son, Ahaziah his son, Joash his son,</p>

<p>%[l|12]&ensp; Amaziah his son, Azariah his son, Jotham his son,</p>

<p>%[l|13]&ensp; Ahaz his son, Hezekiah his son, Manasseh his son,</p>

<p>%[l|14]&ensp; Amon his son, Josiah his son.</p>

<p>%[l|15]&ensp; And the sons of Josiah <i>were</i>, the firstborn Johanan, the second Jehoiakim, the third Zedekiah, the fourth Shallum.</p>

<p>%[l|16]&ensp; And the sons of Jehoiakim: Jeconiah his son, Zedekiah his son.</p>

<p>%[l|17]&ensp; And the sons of Jeconiah; Assir, Salathiel his son,</p>

<p>%[l|18]&ensp; Malchiram also, and Pedaiah, and Shenazar, Jecamiah, Hoshama, and Nedabiah.</p>

<p>%[l|19]&ensp; And the sons of Pedaiah <i>were</i>, Zerubbabel, and Shimei: and the sons of Zerubbabel; Meshullam, and Hananiah, and Shelomith their sister:</p>

<p>%[l|20]&ensp; And Hashubah, and Ohel, and Berechiah, and Hasadiah, Jushabhesed, five.</p>

<p>%[l|21]&ensp; And the sons of Hananiah; Pelatiah, and Jesaiah: the sons of Rephaiah, the sons of Arnan, the sons of Obadiah, the sons of Shechaniah.</p>

<p>%[l|22]&ensp; And the sons of Shechaniah; Shemaiah: and the sons of Shemaiah; Hattush, and Igeal, and Bariah, and Neariah, and Shaphat, six.</p>

<p>%[l|23]&ensp; And the sons of Neariah; Elioenai, and Hezekiah, and Azrikam, three.</p>

<p>%[l|24]&ensp; And the sons of Elioenai <i>were</i>, Hodaiah, and Eliashib, and Pelaiah, and Akkub, and Johanan, and Dalaiah, and Anani, seven.</p>
