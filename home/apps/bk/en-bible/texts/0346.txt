# I Chronicles 8

<p>%[l|1]&ensp; Now Benjamin begat Bela his firstborn, Ashbel the second, and Aharah the third,</p>

<p>%[l|2]&ensp; Nohah the fourth, and Rapha the fifth.</p>

<p>%[l|3]&ensp; And the sons of Bela were, Addar, and Gera, and Abihud,</p>

<p>%[l|4]&ensp; And Abishua, and Naaman, and Ahoah,</p>

<p>%[l|5]&ensp; And Gera, and Shephuphan, and Huram.</p>

<p>%[l|6]&ensp; And these <i>are</i> the sons of Ehud: these are the heads of the fathers of the inhabitants of Geba, and they removed them to Manahath:</p>

<p>%[l|7]&ensp; And Naaman, and Ahiah, and Gera, he removed them, and begat Uzza, and Ahihud.</p>

<p>%[l|8]&ensp; And Shaharaim begat <i>children</i> in the country of Moab, after he had sent them away; Hushim and Baara <i>were</i> his wives.</p>

<p>%[l|9]&ensp; And he begat of Hodesh his wife, Jobab, and Zibia, and Mesha, and Malcham,</p>

<p>%[l|10]&ensp; And Jeuz, and Shachia, and Mirma. These <i>were</i> his sons, heads of the fathers.</p>

<p>%[l|11]&ensp; And of Hushim he begat Abitub, and Elpaal.</p>

<p>%[l|12]&ensp; The sons of Elpaal; Eber, and Misham, and Shamed, who built Ono, and Lod, with the towns thereof:</p>

<p>%[l|13]&ensp; Beriah also, and Shema, who <i>were</i> heads of the fathers of the inhabitants of Aijalon, who drove away the inhabitants of Gath:</p>

<p>%[l|14]&ensp; And Ahio, Shashak, and Jeremoth,</p>

<p>%[l|15]&ensp; And Zebadiah, and Arad, and Ader,</p>

<p>%[l|16]&ensp; And Michael, and Ispah, and Joha, the sons of Beriah;</p>

<p>%[l|17]&ensp; And Zebadiah, and Meshullam, and Hezeki, and Heber,</p>

<p>%[l|18]&ensp; Ishmerai also, and Jezliah, and Jobab, the sons of Elpaal;</p>

<p>%[l|19]&ensp; And Jakim, and Zichri, and Zabdi,</p>

<p>%[l|20]&ensp; And Elienai, and Zilthai, and Eliel,</p>

<p>%[l|21]&ensp; And Adaiah, and Beraiah, and Shimrath, the sons of Shimhi;</p>

<p>%[l|22]&ensp; And Ishpan, and Heber, and Eliel,</p>

<p>%[l|23]&ensp; And Abdon, and Zichri, and Hanan,</p>

<p>%[l|24]&ensp; And Hananiah, and Elam, and Antothijah,</p>

<p>%[l|25]&ensp; And Iphedeiah, and Penuel, the sons of Shashak;</p>

<p>%[l|26]&ensp; And Shamsherai, and Shehariah, and Athaliah,</p>

<p>%[l|27]&ensp; And Jaresiah, and Eliah, and Zichri, the sons of Jeroham.</p>

<p>%[l|28]&ensp; These <i>were</i> heads of the fathers, by their generations, chief <i>men</i>. These dwelt in Jerusalem.</p>

<p>%[l|29]&ensp; And at Gibeon dwelt the father of Gibeon; whose wife's name <i>was</i> Maachah:</p>

<p>%[l|30]&ensp; And his firstborn son Abdon, and Zur, and Kish, and Baal, and Nadab,</p>

<p>%[l|31]&ensp; And Gedor, and Ahio, and Zacher.</p>

<p>%[l|32]&ensp; And Mikloth begat Shimeah. And these also dwelt with their brethren in Jerusalem, over against them.</p>

<p>%[l|33]&ensp; And Ner begat Kish, and Kish begat Saul, and Saul begat Jonathan, and Malchishua, and Abinadab, and Eshbaal.</p>

<p>%[l|34]&ensp; And the son of Jonathan <i>was</i> Meribbaal; and Meribbaal begat Micah.</p>

<p>%[l|35]&ensp; And the sons of Micah <i>were</i>, Pithon, and Melech, and Tarea, and Ahaz.</p>

<p>%[l|36]&ensp; And Ahaz begat Jehoadah; and Jehoadah begat Alemeth, and Azmaveth, and Zimri; and Zimri begat Moza,</p>

<p>%[l|37]&ensp; And Moza begat Binea: Rapha <i>was</i> his son, Eleasah his son, Azel his son:</p>

<p>%[l|38]&ensp; And Azel had six sons, whose names <i>are</i> these, Azrikam, Bocheru, and Ishmael, and Sheariah, and Obadiah, and Hanan. All these <i>were</i> the sons of Azel.</p>

<p>%[l|39]&ensp; And the sons of Eshek his brother <i>were</i>, Ulam his firstborn, Jehush the second, and Eliphelet the third.</p>

<p>%[l|40]&ensp; And the sons of Ulam were mighty men of valour, archers, and had many sons, and sons' sons, an hundred and fifty. All these <i>are</i> of the sons of Benjamin.</p>
