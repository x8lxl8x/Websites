# Psalms 108

<p class="intro">A Song <i>or</i> Psalm of David.</p>

<p>%[l|1]&ensp; O God, my heart is fixed; I will sing and give praise, even with my glory.</p>

<p>%[l|2]&ensp; Awake, psaltery and harp: I <i>myself</i> will awake early.</p>

<p>%[l|3]&ensp; I will praise thee, O LORD, among the people: and I will sing praises unto thee among the nations.</p>

<p>%[l|4]&ensp; For thy mercy <i>is</i> great above the heavens: and thy truth <i>reacheth</i> unto the clouds.</p>

<p>%[l|5]&ensp; Be thou exalted, O God, above the heavens: and thy glory above all the earth;</p>

<p>%[l|6]&ensp; That thy beloved may be delivered: save <i>with</i> thy right hand, and answer me.</p>

<p>%[l|7]&ensp; God hath spoken in his holiness; I will rejoice, I will divide Shechem, and mete out the valley of Succoth.</p>

<p>%[l|8]&ensp; Gilead <i>is</i> mine; Manasseh <i>is</i> mine; Ephraim also <i>is</i> the strength of mine head; Judah <i>is</i> my lawgiver;</p>

<p>%[l|9]&ensp; Moab <i>is</i> my washpot; over Edom will I cast out my shoe; over Philistia will I triumph.</p>

<p>%[l|10]&ensp; Who will bring me into the strong city? who will lead me into Edom?</p>

<p>%[l|11]&ensp; <i>Wilt</i> not <i>thou</i>, O God, <i>who</i> hast cast us off? and wilt not thou, O God, go forth with our hosts?</p>

<p>%[l|12]&ensp; Give us help from trouble: for vain <i>is</i> the help of man.</p>

<p>%[l|13]&ensp; Through God we shall do valiantly: for he <i>it is that</i> shall tread down our enemies.</p>
