# Psalms 138

<p class="intro"><i>A Psalm</i> of David.</p>

<p>%[l|1]&ensp; I will praise thee with my whole heart: before the gods will I sing praise unto thee.</p>

<p>%[l|2]&ensp; I will worship toward thy holy temple, and praise thy name for thy lovingkindness and for thy truth: for thou hast magnified thy word above all thy name.</p>

<p>%[l|3]&ensp; In the day when I cried thou answeredst me, <i>and</i> strengthenedst me <i>with</i> strength in my soul.</p>

<p>%[l|4]&ensp; All the kings of the earth shall praise thee, O LORD, when they hear the words of thy mouth.</p>

<p>%[l|5]&ensp; Yea, they shall sing in the ways of the LORD: for great <i>is</i> the glory of the LORD.</p>

<p>%[l|6]&ensp; Though the LORD <i>be</i> high, yet hath he respect unto the lowly: but the proud he knoweth afar off.</p>

<p>%[l|7]&ensp; Though I walk in the midst of trouble, thou wilt revive me: thou shalt stretch forth thine hand against the wrath of mine enemies, and thy right hand shall save me.</p>

<p>%[l|8]&ensp; The LORD will perfect <i>that which</i> concerneth me: thy mercy, O LORD, <i>endureth</i> for ever: forsake not the works of thine own hands.</p>
