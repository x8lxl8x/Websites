# Psalms 134

<p class="intro">A Song of degrees.</p>

<p>%[l|1]&ensp; Behold, bless ye the LORD, all <i>ye</i> servants of the LORD, which by night stand in the house of the LORD.</p>

<p>%[l|2]&ensp; Lift up your hands <i>in</i> the sanctuary, and bless the LORD.</p>

<p>%[l|3]&ensp; The LORD that made heaven and earth bless thee out of Zion.</p>
