# Genesis 5

<p>%[l|1]&ensp; This <i>is</i> the book of the generations of Adam. In the day that God created man, in the likeness of God made he him;</p>

<p>%[l|2]&ensp; Male and female created he them; and blessed them, and called their name Adam, in the day when they were created.</p>

<p>%[l|3]&ensp; And Adam lived an hundred and thirty years, and begat <i>a son</i> in his own likeness, after his image; and called his name Seth:</p>

<p>%[l|4]&ensp; And the days of Adam after he had begotten Seth were eight hundred years: and he begat sons and daughters:</p>

<p>%[l|5]&ensp; And all the days that Adam lived were nine hundred and thirty years: and he died.</p>

<p>%[l|6]&ensp; And Seth lived an hundred and five years, and begat Enos:</p>

<p>%[l|7]&ensp; And Seth lived after he begat Enos eight hundred and seven years, and begat sons and daughters:</p>

<p>%[l|8]&ensp; And all the days of Seth were nine hundred and twelve years: and he died.</p>

<p>%[l|9]&ensp; And Enos lived ninety years, and begat Cainan:</p>

<p>%[l|10]&ensp; And Enos lived after he begat Cainan eight hundred and fifteen years, and begat sons and daughters:</p>

<p>%[l|11]&ensp; And all the days of Enos were nine hundred and five years: and he died.</p>

<p>%[l|12]&ensp; And Cainan lived seventy years, and begat Mahalaleel:</p>

<p>%[l|13]&ensp; And Cainan lived after he begat Mahalaleel eight hundred and forty years, and begat sons and daughters:</p>

<p>%[l|14]&ensp; And all the days of Cainan were nine hundred and ten years: and he died.</p>

<p>%[l|15]&ensp; And Mahalaleel lived sixty and five years, and begat Jared:</p>

<p>%[l|16]&ensp; And Mahalaleel lived after he begat Jared eight hundred and thirty years, and begat sons and daughters:</p>

<p>%[l|17]&ensp; And all the days of Mahalaleel were eight hundred ninety and five years: and he died.</p>

<p>%[l|18]&ensp; And Jared lived an hundred sixty and two years, and he begat Enoch:</p>

<p>%[l|19]&ensp; And Jared lived after he begat Enoch eight hundred years, and begat sons and daughters:</p>

<p>%[l|20]&ensp; And all the days of Jared were nine hundred sixty and two years: and he died.</p>

<p>%[l|21]&ensp; And Enoch lived sixty and five years, and begat Methuselah:</p>

<p>%[l|22]&ensp; And Enoch walked with God after he begat Methuselah three hundred years, and begat sons and daughters:</p>

<p>%[l|23]&ensp; And all the days of Enoch were three hundred sixty and five years:</p>

<p>%[l|24]&ensp; And Enoch walked with God: and he <i>was</i> not; for God took him.</p>

<p>%[l|25]&ensp; And Methuselah lived an hundred eighty and seven years, and begat Lamech:</p>

<p>%[l|26]&ensp; And Methuselah lived after he begat Lamech seven hundred eighty and two years, and begat sons and daughters:</p>

<p>%[l|27]&ensp; And all the days of Methuselah were nine hundred sixty and nine years: and he died.</p>

<p>%[l|28]&ensp; And Lamech lived an hundred eighty and two years, and begat a son:</p>

<p>%[l|29]&ensp; And he called his name Noah, saying, This <i>same</i> shall comfort us concerning our work and toil of our hands, because of the ground which the LORD hath cursed.</p>

<p>%[l|30]&ensp; And Lamech lived after he begat Noah five hundred ninety and five years, and begat sons and daughters:</p>

<p>%[l|31]&ensp; And all the days of Lamech were seven hundred seventy and seven years: and he died.</p>

<p>%[l|32]&ensp; And Noah was five hundred years old: and Noah begat Shem, Ham, and Japheth.</p>
