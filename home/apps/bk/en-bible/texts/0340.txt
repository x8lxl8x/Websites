# I Chronicles 2

<p>%[l|1]&ensp; These <i>are</i> the sons of Israel; Reuben, Simeon, Levi, and Judah, Issachar, and Zebulun,</p>

<p>%[l|2]&ensp; Dan, Joseph, and Benjamin, Naphtali, Gad, and Asher.</p>

<p>%[l|3]&ensp; The sons of Judah; Er, and Onan, and Shelah: <i>which</i> three were born unto him of the daughter of Shua the Canaanitess. And Er, the firstborn of Judah, was evil in the sight of the LORD; and he slew him.</p>

<p>%[l|4]&ensp; And Tamar his daughter in law bare him Pharez and Zerah. All the sons of Judah <i>were</i> five.</p>

<p>%[l|5]&ensp; The sons of Pharez; Hezron, and Hamul.</p>

<p>%[l|6]&ensp; And the sons of Zerah; Zimri, and Ethan, and Heman, and Calcol, and Dara: five of them in all.</p>

<p>%[l|7]&ensp; And the sons of Carmi; Achar, the troubler of Israel, who transgressed in the thing accursed.</p>

<p>%[l|8]&ensp; And the sons of Ethan; Azariah.</p>

<p>%[l|9]&ensp; The sons also of Hezron, that were born unto him; Jerahmeel, and Ram, and Chelubai.</p>

<p>%[l|10]&ensp; And Ram begat Amminadab; and Amminadab begat Nahshon, prince of the children of Judah;</p>

<p>%[l|11]&ensp; And Nahshon begat Salma, and Salma begat Boaz,</p>

<p>%[l|12]&ensp; And Boaz begat Obed, and Obed begat Jesse,</p>

<p>%[l|13]&ensp; And Jesse begat his firstborn Eliab, and Abinadab the second, and Shimma the third,</p>

<p>%[l|14]&ensp; Nethaneel the fourth, Raddai the fifth,</p>

<p>%[l|15]&ensp; Ozem the sixth, David the seventh:</p>

<p>%[l|16]&ensp; Whose sisters <i>were</i> Zeruiah, and Abigail. And the sons of Zeruiah; Abishai, and Joab, and Asahel, three.</p>

<p>%[l|17]&ensp; And Abigail bare Amasa: and the father of Amasa <i>was</i> Jether the Ishmeelite.</p>

<p>%[l|18]&ensp; And Caleb the son of Hezron begat <i>children</i> of Azubah <i>his</i> wife, and of Jerioth: her sons <i>are</i> these; Jesher, and Shobab, and Ardon.</p>

<p>%[l|19]&ensp; And when Azubah was dead, Caleb took unto him Ephrath, which bare him Hur.</p>

<p>%[l|20]&ensp; And Hur begat Uri, and Uri begat Bezaleel.</p>

<p>%[l|21]&ensp; And afterward Hezron went in to the daughter of Machir the father of Gilead, whom he married when he <i>was</i> threescore years old; and she bare him Segub.</p>

<p>%[l|22]&ensp; And Segub begat Jair, who had three and twenty cities in the land of Gilead.</p>

<p>%[l|23]&ensp; And he took Geshur, and Aram, with the towns of Jair, from them, with Kenath, and the towns thereof, <i>even</i> threescore cities. All these <i>belonged to</i> the sons of Machir the father of Gilead.</p>

<p>%[l|24]&ensp; And after that Hezron was dead in Calebephratah, then Abiah Hezron's wife bare him Ashur the father of Tekoa.</p>

<p>%[l|25]&ensp; And the sons of Jerahmeel the firstborn of Hezron were, Ram the firstborn, and Bunah, and Oren, and Ozem, <i>and</i> Ahijah.</p>

<p>%[l|26]&ensp; Jerahmeel had also another wife, whose name <i>was</i> Atarah; she <i>was</i> the mother of Onam.</p>

<p>%[l|27]&ensp; And the sons of Ram the firstborn of Jerahmeel were, Maaz, and Jamin, and Eker.</p>

<p>%[l|28]&ensp; And the sons of Onam were, Shammai, and Jada. And the sons of Shammai; Nadab, and Abishur.</p>

<p>%[l|29]&ensp; And the name of the wife of Abishur <i>was</i> Abihail, and she bare him Ahban, and Molid.</p>

<p>%[l|30]&ensp; And the sons of Nadab; Seled, and Appaim: but Seled died without children.</p>

<p>%[l|31]&ensp; And the sons of Appaim; Ishi. And the sons of Ishi; Sheshan. And the children of Sheshan; Ahlai.</p>

<p>%[l|32]&ensp; And the sons of Jada the brother of Shammai; Jether, and Jonathan: and Jether died without children.</p>

<p>%[l|33]&ensp; And the sons of Jonathan; Peleth, and Zaza. These were the sons of Jerahmeel.</p>

<p>%[l|34]&ensp; Now Sheshan had no sons, but daughters. And Sheshan had a servant, an Egyptian, whose name <i>was</i> Jarha.</p>

<p>%[l|35]&ensp; And Sheshan gave his daughter to Jarha his servant to wife; and she bare him Attai.</p>

<p>%[l|36]&ensp; And Attai begat Nathan, and Nathan begat Zabad,</p>

<p>%[l|37]&ensp; And Zabad begat Ephlal, and Ephlal begat Obed,</p>

<p>%[l|38]&ensp; And Obed begat Jehu, and Jehu begat Azariah,</p>

<p>%[l|39]&ensp; And Azariah begat Helez, and Helez begat Eleasah,</p>

<p>%[l|40]&ensp; And Eleasah begat Sisamai, and Sisamai begat Shallum,</p>

<p>%[l|41]&ensp; And Shallum begat Jekamiah, and Jekamiah begat Elishama.</p>

<p>%[l|42]&ensp; Now the sons of Caleb the brother of Jerahmeel <i>were</i>, Mesha his firstborn, which was the father of Ziph; and the sons of Mareshah the father of Hebron.</p>

<p>%[l|43]&ensp; And the sons of Hebron; Korah, and Tappuah, and Rekem, and Shema.</p>

<p>%[l|44]&ensp; And Shema begat Raham, the father of Jorkoam: and Rekem begat Shammai.</p>

<p>%[l|45]&ensp; And the son of Shammai <i>was</i> Maon: and Maon <i>was</i> the father of Bethzur.</p>

<p>%[l|46]&ensp; And Ephah, Caleb's concubine, bare Haran, and Moza, and Gazez: and Haran begat Gazez.</p>

<p>%[l|47]&ensp; And the sons of Jahdai; Regem, and Jotham, and Geshan, and Pelet, and Ephah, and Shaaph.</p>

<p>%[l|48]&ensp; Maachah, Caleb's concubine, bare Sheber, and Tirhanah.</p>

<p>%[l|49]&ensp; She bare also Shaaph the father of Madmannah, Sheva the father of Machbenah, and the father of Gibea: and the daughter of Caleb <i>was</i> Achsah.</p>

<p>%[l|50]&ensp; These were the sons of Caleb the son of Hur, the firstborn of Ephratah; Shobal the father of Kirjathjearim,</p>

<p>%[l|51]&ensp; Salma the father of Bethlehem, Hareph the father of Bethgader.</p>

<p>%[l|52]&ensp; And Shobal the father of Kirjathjearim had sons; Haroeh, <i>and</i> half of the Manahethites.</p>

<p>%[l|53]&ensp; And the families of Kirjathjearim; the Ithrites, and the Puhites, and the Shumathites, and the Mishraites; of them came the Zareathites, and the Eshtaulites.</p>

<p>%[l|54]&ensp; The sons of Salma; Bethlehem, and the Netophathites, Ataroth, the house of Joab, and half of the Manahethites, the Zorites.</p>

<p>%[l|55]&ensp; And the families of the scribes which dwelt at Jabez; the Tirathites, the Shimeathites, <i>and</i> Suchathites. These <i>are</i> the Kenites that came of Hemath, the father of the house of Rechab.</p>
